Just need a jar?

Try out the nightly build:
  • meshkeeper-api-1.0-SNAPSHOT.jar

Are you a Maven User?

Just the following to your pom.xml

<dependency>
  <groupId>org.fusesource.meshkeeper</groupId>
  <artifactId>meshkeeper-api</artifactId>
  <version>1.0-SNAPSHOT</version>
</dependency>

… and also our repository …

  
<repositories>
  <repository>
    <id>meshkeeper.release.m2</id>
    <name>meshkeeper Release Repository</name>
    <url>http://meshkeeper.fusesource.org/repo/release</url>
    <snapshots>
      <enabled>false</enabled>
    </snapshots>
    <releases>
      <enabled>true</enabled>
    </releases>
  </repository>
  <repository>
    <id>meshkeeper.snapshot.m2</id>
    <name>meshkeeper Release Repository</name>
    <url>http://meshkeeper.fusesource.org/repo/snapshot</url>
    <snapshots>
      <enabled>true</enabled>
    </snapshots>
    <releases>
      <enabled>false</enabled>
    </releases>
  </repository>
</repositories>
